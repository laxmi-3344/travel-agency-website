Popular Trekking Destinations in Nepal

Everest Base Camp

Experience the world’s highest peak up close with our guided Everest Base Camp trek. Suitable for experienced trekkers.

Annapurna Circuit

Discover diverse landscapes and cultures on the Annapurna Circuit trek, one of the most popular in Nepal.

Mustang

Explore the mystical kingdom of Mustang, where ancient Tibetan culture meets stunning Himalayan desert landscapes. Discover colorful monasteries, centuries-old caves, and remote villages that offer a truly unforgettable adventure..

Langtang Valley

Langtang Valley – A beautiful trek close to Kathmandu, known for its rhododendron forests and Himalayan views. It offers rich Tamang culture, diverse wildlife, and serene mountain villages. Perfect for those seeking both natural beauty and cultural immersion..

Jomsom Muktinath

Pilgrims visit to bathe in the holy waters and seek blessings, while travelers enjoy its unique blend of culture, religion, and Himalayan beauty.

Pokhara

Discover Pokhara with us – a peaceful lakeside paradise where the snow-capped Annapurna range meets the calm waters of Phewa Lake. We’ll guide you through scenic boat rides, thrilling paragliding adventures, hidden caves, and breathtaking sunrise views from Sarangkot.
